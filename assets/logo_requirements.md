# Driped Logo Requirements

Status labels:
- `existing-core`: already represented in the current app catalog and should stay in the new logo set.
- `add-next`: not in the current catalog or not fully surfaced yet, but high-value and worth adding.
- `rare-keep`: lower-traffic or niche brands that are still useful enough to keep available.
